LIST OF BRICKS BELIEVED TO HAVE BEEN MANUFACTURED IN CHELTENHAM/DOGTOWN BRICKS

The column heads mean the following:

  • BRAND: The name of the brand that is stamped on the brick.
  • WHERE IS EVIDENCE OF ITS EXISTENCE FOUND:
    • GR = Graves' book.
    • GU = Gurcke's book.
    • ME = Not in either book, but in my collection.
  • PAGE = On what page in Graves or Gurcke.
  • COMPANY = Which Cheltenham / Dogtown brick maker made the brand.
  • DATE = About when was this particular type brick made.
