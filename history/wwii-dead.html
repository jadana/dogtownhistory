DOGTOWN PEOPLE WHO DIED IN WORLD WAR II

This is a page devoted to hornoring and listing those from Dogtown who have lost their lives in wars.

Currently I only have a list of known World War II victims.

I would ask any of your who know other WWII victims, or Dogtowners from OTHER wars who died in service, please send these names to me.

I also invite and welcome photos of these people and or short biographical date, or stories of their service in the war.
