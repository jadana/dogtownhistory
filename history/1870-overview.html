1870 U.S. Federal Census ---------- Central Township ---------- St. Louis County, Missouri

Microfilm copies of this census are available to researchers without charge at three locations: The Missouri Historical Society on Skinker Blvd., The St. Louis City Library, main branch, on Olive Blvd., and the Main Branch of the St. Louis County Library on S. Lindbergh Rd.

The items presented are:

  1. Dwellings #
  2. Families #
  3. Last Name
  4. First Name
  5. Age
  6. Gender
  7. Race
  8. Profession, Occupation or trade data is listed.
  9. Place of Birth.
  10. The page number on the census records.
