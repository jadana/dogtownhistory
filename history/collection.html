BOB CORBETT'S COLLECTION OF CHELTENHAM/DOGTOWN BRICKS

LAST UPDATED: July 3, 2002

Of the 199 brands thought to have been made by Dogtown brick factories I have 27 of them at present.

There are two authoritative lists of brick brands known. These are:
  1. Gurcke, Karl. BRICKS AND BRICKMAKING: A HANDBOOK FOR HISTORICAL ARCHAEOLOGY Moscow, Idaho: The University of Idaho Press, 1987.

  2. Graves, Jim. BRICK BRANDS OF THE UNITED STATES. Compiled by Jim Graves, September 5, 2000. Unpublished.

In the chart of known bricks and of my own collection I refer to these books as:

  1. GR = Graves' book.
  2. GU = Gurcke's book.
  3. ME = Not in either book, but in my collection. There are several charts to look at:

    • List of the brick brands believed to have been manufactured in Dogtown/Cheltenham.

    • Bob Corbett's own existing collection of Dogtown/Cheltenham bricks: You may view this in three different ways:
      • Listed alphabetically by brick brand name.
      • Listed alphabetically by the brick company which made the bricks
      • Listed numerically by Corbett's numbering system. This is a specially useful list were you coming to see Corbett's brick collection. Each brick has a number on it. This list would enable you to then look up that particular brick.
