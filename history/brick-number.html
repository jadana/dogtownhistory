BOB CORBETT'S BRICK COLLECTION LIST BY BRICK NUMBER

List compiled by Bob Corbett

All bricks in this collection are believed to have been manufactured in Dogtown

This particular list is arranged the display number I have on the brick in my own collection.

There are two other lists of the bricks

  • Bricks listed alphabetically by brand name

  • Bricks listed alphabetically by brick factory which made the brick

SPECIAL NOTES: Bricks listed with a number and letter (eg. BR 7A) indicates this brick is a variation of that basic brand name. Companies often used the same brand name but slightly changed the design of the brick. Also, slashes in the listing (eg) BR 1 ACME / 48 -57), means the 48 - 57 is on a different line than the Acme. Each slash indicates a new line.
