BIBLIOGRAHY OF ST. LOUIS AREA BOOKS WHICH HAVE NO USEFUL INFORMATION ON THIS AREA

This section is to save others work. I've checked each of the following works with some care and found them to have nothing of interest for people researching the areas of Dogtown, Cheltenham and close by areas. You can save time and energy and turn to other works!

  • Hyde, William and Howard L. Conrad, editors.  ENCYCLOPEDIA OF THE HISTORY OF ST. LOUIS: A COMPENDIUM OF HISTORY AND BIOGRAPHY FOR READY REFERENCE. VOLUME I.   New York: The Southern History Co., 1899.

  • Kirschten, Ernest.   CATFISH AND CRYSTAL,   Garden City, NY: Doubleday and Co., Inc., 1960.

  • Lipsitz, George.   THE SIDEWALKS OF ST. LOUIS: PLACES, PEOPLE, AND POLITICS IN AN AMERICAN CITY.    Columbia, MO: University of Missouri Press, 1991.

  • Stadler, Frances Hurd.  ST. LOUIS: A HISTORY OF THE CITY FROM ITS FOUNDING TO THE EVE OF ITS TWO HUNDREDTH ANNIVERSARY.   St. Louis: Radio KSD and Kriegshauser Mortuaries, 1963.

  • Stevens, Walter B.  ST. LOUIS: THE FOURTH CITY: 1764-1909. VOLUME I    St. Louis: The S.J. Clarke Publishing Co., 1909.

  • Wayman, Norbury L.  A PICTORIAL HISTORY OF ST. LOUIS    St. Louis: 1968. Publisher not listed.
