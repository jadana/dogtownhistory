THE BARS OF DOGTOWN: THE PILOT HOUSE AND ALL IT'S NAMES

John Corbett helps straighten out some of the names of this long-time tavern:

Actually, Pat Liston wrote that Liston's Dogtown Pub was the old Pilot House Bar at 1221 Tamm. Dorothy's Lounge was afterwards named Novacks, then Mister's and then Rosies, and is closed for good. The neighbors decided it was time for a permanent change and petitioned for it's demise.
