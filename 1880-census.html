1880 UNITED STATES FEDERAL CENSUS

A huge thanks is due Sally Sharamitaro who undertook this difficult task of FINDING and transcribing this census for our use. I say a difficulty in "finding" since much of this census does not list people by street address, they had none, but only by area. Since the area being canvassed was considerably larger that even our current larger Dogtown area, Sally was forced to do a great deal of detective work and make hard decisions about who actually lived in the Dogtown area and who did. It was a very difficult task!

    This data is list only by last name since the addresses when at all avaialble are not of the sort that most people would use to search specific persons. The last name search is the most useful for this census.
