HISTORY OF ST. JAMES PARISH

Speaker: Bob Corbett
Date: July 12, 2003
Time: 7:00 PM
Place: St. James Gym

From the minutes of the DHS Speakers Series Events.

The third in our DHS Speaker Series Events, “The History of St. James Parish,” was held on June 22, 2003 in the St. James School Auditorium. Forty nine people signed our attendance sheets. Bob Corbett traced the history of St. James Parish from its beginnings as a mission of St. Malach’s Parish in 1860 through it’s 90 years of Irish-born pastors.
