"HISTORY OF THE MUNY OPERA

Date: Sunday, February 19th, 2006
Time: 7:00 PM
Place: St. James School cafeteria, 1360 Tamm Ave.



Admission is free and refreshments will be served.

The Muny.... If you grew up in St. Louis, you have your own special memories of the first time you heard the “music under the stars”!

BUT... have you ever wondered about the history of the Mu.ny? How it began on that hillside in Forest Park? How it grew to become the oldest and largest outdoor theatre In the country?

Laura Peters Reilly, our speaker, and the Muny Opera’s Director of Marketing since 1992, grew up In St. Louis and knows The Muny as a St. Louis tradiffon. She will share with us a slide show that traces the history of THE MUNY from its humble beginnings and closes with a “Cavalcade of Stars”.

The Dogtown Historical Society invites you to come and enjoy this wonderful evening with us!

The Dogtown Historical Society is a non-profit organization established in Missouri in 2002.
