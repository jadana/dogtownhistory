THE FAMILY OF JACOB TAMM



  • From: Notable St. Louis Genealogies

    Jacob Tamm was born in Germany. He died in Oct 1892. He married Julia Schraeder on 9 Mar 1841.

    Julia Schraeder died about 1891. She married Jacob Tamm on 9 Mar 1841.

    They had the following children:

    • M i Maximillian Tamm was born on 1 Feb 1853 in St. Louis, Missouri.
    • M ii Theodore Tamm.
