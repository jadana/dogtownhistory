NATURE'S MEDICINE

By: Julie Duane



As a weary soul of the quickened pace

I experienced today a sweet panacea

A oneness with the maple trees

The gentle wind sloughing through the leaves

And rippling softly through the branches

Wiping away my queries and doubts

Restoring serenity, giving me pause

To marvel at nature’s healing powers
