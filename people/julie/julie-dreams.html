SWEET DREAMS

By: Julie Duane



I dreamt of mama again last night

As I have so often through the years

I woke to the early morning light

And knew that today there'd be no tears

There'd be no problems I couldn't face

And my little world would be all right

A happier and a better place

Because I dreamt of MAMA last night
