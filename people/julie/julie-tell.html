WHAT NOT TO TELL ME

By: Julie Duane





You may tell me "No, you shouldn't"

I may answer, "Oh, I wouldn't"

But don't tell me "You can't do that"

I'll accept no negative format

It will only spur me on to try –

And I will do it or I'll die
