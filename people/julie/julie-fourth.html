FOURTH OF JULY

By: Julie Duane

On the fourth of July when I was young

The stores all closed and the flag was hung

From every porch; ice cream was made

While we had picnics in the shade

Kinfolks stopped by, and friends we knew

We had firecrackers and spit-devils too

Then when it got dark we screamed with delight

As we ran waving sparklers around in the night
