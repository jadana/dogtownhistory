ON GROWNING OLD

By: Julie Duane



Don't tell me to grow old gracefully

I find that unacceptable

Having never given age one thought

For all my many youthful years

I'm aware that I am growing old

But there's one thing you must understand

I'll do it the way I've done everything else;

In whatever manner I damn well please
