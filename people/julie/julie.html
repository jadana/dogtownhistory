THE WRITINGS OF JULIE DUANE, POET

Julie moves to nursing home
Folks, Julie recently (summer 2005) moved into a nursing home. If you read her poetry and would like to communicate with her, she would appreciate it a great deal. She's at:

Brook View Nursing Home
2963 Doddridge
Maryland Heights, MO 63043
314-291-4557



Corbett notes: Julie Duane is my Tamm Ave. neighbor. She is also a wonderful poet of both humerous and serious poetry. Julie was born in 1920 in HUmboldt, Tennessee. At age 17 she began playing the piano and singing in a trio with her two sisters. Later she moved on to play with many different bands, playing every sort of music from 1930s-40s, to jazz, modern pop, even rock and roll. Julie, now 82 years of (2003) still plays "gigs" around St. Louis.

Julie has lived in Dogtown the past 24 years.
