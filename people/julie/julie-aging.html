CHANT FOR AGING MUSICIANS
(written by one who is)

By: Julie Duane

No diminished chords for growing older

Try augmented and getting bolder

Don't tiptoe around with pianissimo

Step out boldly with fortissimo

And when the final bars are pending

Delay them with a double ending
