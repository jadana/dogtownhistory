SOME "I LIKES"

By: Julie Duane



I like rainbows

And jazzy clothes

All kinds of cats

And flowery hats

I like airplanes

And country lanes

Soft chords that please

In minor keys

All kinds of boats

Mardi Gras floats

The St. Louis Cards

Lace by the yards

And folks who take times,

To read my rhymes
