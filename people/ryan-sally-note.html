SALLY RYAN SHARAMITARO

FIRST HONORARY DOGTOWN CITIZEN

Sally has never actually lived in Dogtown. However, she is not only a charter member of the Dogtown historical society, and the current secretary of the organization in her second term of office, but one of the most contributing members of the society since the beginning.

It seemed to me only fitting that I include her in the category of Dogtown resident even though she's never actually resided here. Her contribution to this history is exceptional and she is well worthy of this honorary title.

Bob Corbett
April 19, 2006
