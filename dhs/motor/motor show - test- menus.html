THE 2011 DOGTOWN STREET MUSICIANS FESTIVAL
SEPTEMBER 17, 2011

This second annual Dogtown Street Musician's Festival was held at the intersection of Tamm and Clayton



THE 2011 FESTIVAL IS DEDICATED TO - NICK MUCCI


The Second Annual Dogtown Street Musician's Festival is dedicated to Nick Mucci, a professional musician, member of the St. Louis Jazz Club and 52 year resident of the Dogtown neighborhood.


The dedication plaque reads: "The DHS is proud to dedicate the Second Annual Dogtown Street Musicians Festival, September 17th, 2011, to Nick Mucci, who has shared his marvelous musical talent by entertaining others for so many years. We are pleased to honor this great Dogtown musician".
