THE DOGTOWN HISTORICAL SOCIETY MEMORIAL BENCHES

HOW TO GET A BENCH FOR YOUR SPECIAL FOLKS

  • Who may be remembered with benches?

    Anyone who actually lived in Dogtown.

  • How can you get a bench?

    E-mail Bob Corbett at the address below to inquire about a place for a bench, and reserving that space.

    We must have the property owner's permission to place a bench adjacent to or on their property, and we must have a St. Louis City permit.

    Once the place has been chosen and the property owner agrees, then we will seek the appropriate permit.

  • What do the benches look like and the attached plaques?

    See page on the benches already in place.

  • What is the cost of a bench?

    Currently benches cost $500.00. This is for the bench, plaque, permit and insurance.

  • Whom do I contact?

    Bob Corbett corbetre@webster.edu
