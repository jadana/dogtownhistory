DEWEY SCHOOL CLASS OF 1935

Thanks to Mrs. Hefele for the loan of the Jenneman photograph and scout photo below

The school photos were sent to me by Robert Messel.

Graduation class from Dewey School, June 1935.

Robert Messel writes: "Wilma Dilcher Messel, is the third from the left in the second row. She told me that they went across Clayton to the yard of a neighbor who had a nice garden to take the picture.... Mom said the photographer took forever to take the shot and they were “holding smiles”. Everyone got tired by the time the shot was taken and many looked like they had frowns."

Wilma Dilcher Messel at her Dewey graduation, June 1935.

Wilma Dilcher Messel in 1st or 2nd grade from Dewey School, About 1930 or earlier.

Robert Messel writes: "Mom said the picture was taken in Forest Park, which was directly across Clayton from the school."

Class of 1935, Kindergarden photo.

Robert Messel writes: "My mother is blonde girl almost dead center. She is the fourth from the left in the second row. She said that the tall girl on the left is Carol Taylor. The 2nd boy from the left is Jenneman, who has pictures on the web site. The last two boys on the right are Bob Burns and Leroy Thompson.

Class of 1935, Kindergarden photo.

Bob Corbett notes: I included this photo, the full photo of the one I cropped above, to give the effect of the original photo.
