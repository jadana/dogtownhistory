DEWEY SCHOOL CLASS OF 1927

There were two classes, January and June 1927. Below is the class list of the January class list.

Beneath that is the class list for June 1927, including the later very famous Harry Carey (Harry Carabina at the time), as well as some photos provided by Nancy Phelan. These were photos of her mother, Alene Richardson Phelan, member of the class.
