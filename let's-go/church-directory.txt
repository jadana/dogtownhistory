<p align="center"><strong>Masses</strong></p>
<p>Sunday and New Year's Day -- 6.00, 7:30, 9:00, 10:00.<br>
Holidays -- 6:00, 7:30, 9:00.<br>
Week Days -- 6:30 and 8:15 during Lent and the Months of May and October.<br>
<p align="center"><strong>Holy Hour </strong></p>
<p>Thursday before First Friday at 7:30 P.M.
<p align="center"><strong>Instructions for Public School Children </strong></p>
<p>Fridays during school year at 4 P. M., and every Sunday after 9 o'clock Mass.
<p align="center"><strong>Confessions</strong></p>
<p>Saturdays from 4:30 to 6:00 P.M. and 7:30 to 9:00 P. M.<br>
Sunday mornings from 7:00 to 7:25 A. M. for the aged and those who are not free to go on Saturdays.<br>
On the eve of solemn festivals and First Fridays -- 3:30 to 4:30 and from 7:30 to 8:30 P. M.<br>
Special confessors on Eves of Christmas and Easter.
<p align="center"><strong>Meetings</strong></p>
<p>Parish meetings the last Thursday of each month at 8 P. M. All invited.<br>
Mother's Club meeting first Friday of each month at 2:30 P. M.<br>
Virgin Mother's Sodality -- Tuesday after first Sunday at 8:00 P. M.<br>
Holy Name Society -- Monday after 2nd Sunday of the month at 8:00 P. M.<br>
St. Anne’s Sodality -- ,Tuesday after 3rd Sun. day at8:00P.M.

<p align="center"><strong>Visiting Hours to Rectory </strong></p>
<p>Preferably from 8 to 10 A.M. and from 6:30 to 8:00 P. M.

<p align="center"><strong>Baptisms</strong></p>
<p>Sundays at 3:30 P. M. sharp. Sponsors ought to be practicing Catholics.

<p align="center"><strong>Sick Calls</strong></p>
<p>Don't delay; the pastors will not be responsible for sick calls over the telephone. In all cases confer with one of the Priests. 
<p align="center"><strong>Bells</strong></p>
<p>Church bells are rung for the Angelus at 6.00 A. M., 12:00 and 6:00 P. M. Also a quarter of an hour before services.
<p align="center"><strong>Church Dues</strong></p>
<p>Each wage earner is expected to give 4c out of each dollar earned; for maintenance of priests. sisters, ordinary parish expense and for a building fund. No pew rent or special collections except at Christmas and Easter.
