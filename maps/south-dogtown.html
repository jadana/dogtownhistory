DOGTOWN -- SOUTH OF MANCHESTER

For the purposes of the century home committee I'd ask you to consider the maps below. Were we to include the street in between Hampton and McCauland and Manchester on the north and a line that followed I-44 from Hampton to Southwest and then Southwest to McCausland then we would include the following streets:

  • Knox
  • Eveline St.
  • Adeline St.
  • Knox Industrial Ct.
  • Famous
  • Wilson
  • Bischoff Pl.
  • Ecoff
  • Balson
  • Magnolia
  • Noonan

There are no street between these two maps, but there is space.

The primary argument for this inclusion is that when those century houses were built this was definitely a socially connected area to Dogtown.

What do you think?
