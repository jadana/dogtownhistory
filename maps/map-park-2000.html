MAP OF FOREST PARK IN THE YEAR 2000

The map is prior to the massive renovation of the park in preparation for 2004.

The numbers on the map correspond to:

  1. The Boat House
  2. Cabanne House
  3. The Cricket Field
  4. Dwight Davis Tennis Courts
  5. The Nine-hole Golf Course
  6. The Grand Basin
  7. The Jewel Box
  8. Missouri Historical Society Library (on Skinnker)
  9. Missouri Historical Museum (Jefferson Memorial)
    and The Science Center
  10. The Muny Opera
  11. The St. Louis Art Museum
  12. The Planetarium
  13. The St. Louis Zoo
  14. Steinberg Skating Rink
  15. Triple A Golf and Tennis Club
  16. Turtle Park
  17. The World's Fair Pavilion
