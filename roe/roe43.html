ROE SCHOOL CLASS OF JANUARY 1943

37 students
17 girls and 20 boys

Special thanks to Don Flynne for both photo and the names and the wonderful autograph book!

Photo is from January 27, 1943, taken in front of Roe School on Prather Ave.

Class names and other data are beneath photo

Be sure to see the photos from the autograph book below the class photo.

Top row: 1. Delores McNabb, 2. Beverly Chamberlain, 3. Katherine Syzmanski, 4. John Warner, 5. Marian Werner, 6. Joe Fetzer, 7. UNKNOWN, 8. Jack Overturf, 9. Dorothy Parker, 10. George Washington.

2nd row from top: Ms. Hedinger, Eighth Grade Teacher, 1. Dewey Rauls, 2. Donald Shultz, 3. Sam Pizzo, 4. Ralph Bedwell, 5. Frank Bayless, 6. Theresa Cerleggio, 7. Wilbur Smith, Miss Baer, Principal, 8. UNKNOWN.

2nd row from bottom: 1. Robert McBride, 2. UNKNOWN, 3. LaVern Pigg, 4. Burl Wamack, 5. Betty Oliver, 6. John Maloney, 7. Shirley Hess, 8. George Donald, 9. Betty Emory, 10. Melvin Metyia, 11. Jane Oliver.

Bottom row: 1. Vincent Jennemann, 2. Vera Rook, 3. David Weiland, 4. Betty Bavies, 5. Robert Thill, 6. Betty Kuhlman, 7. Don Flynn, 8. Bett Pizzo.

Don Flynn adds: Here’s the Roe School photo. Three I can’t remember. One girl in the top row, one girl in the second row, and a girl at the right end of the third row next to Ms. Baer, the principal. I think she is a Warner, sister of John Warner, but am not sure and can’t remember her first name.
