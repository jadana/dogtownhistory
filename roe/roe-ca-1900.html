ROE SCHOOL BUILDING ABOUT 1900

On Mitchell near Prather.

The first location of Roe School was listed as:

  • 1888 -- Benton Station
  • 1889 -- Renamed as Roe School
  • 1896 -- Damaged by tornado

The building pictured above was demolished in 1940 and the present (2003) school was opened in 1921.

The school is named for John J. Roe (1809-1870) steamboat captain and businessman.
