  • Brooks, George R.  "SOME NEW VIEWS OF OLD CHELTENHAM,"   THE BULLETIN OF THE MISSOURI HISTORICAL SOCIETY 22 (October 1965), pp. 32-34.
    Rich and useful article upon the occasion of the Historical Society coming into possession of several watercolors of the Cheltenham area. Several of the paintings are pictured in black and white with historical details and comparisons with contemporary photographs of the area.  I have only a xerox copy of this work and given the lovely photographs.
