  • Did Charles Lindberg actually live in Dogtown, down on Lloyd Ave. as some have said? If so this needs to be documented and evidenced.

    Bill Vorbeck adds:

    Regarding Charles Lindbergh and other prominent citizens who visited St. Louis --

    My mother Mary J. Vorbeck typed a manuscript for a women, retired School Teacher, "Titled Prominent People who've Visited St. Louis. She had written other books on similar subjects.

    I can't for the life of me remember her name. But She, Mrs. McVey and Patty (all deceased) and my mother lived in the same building in the Plaza Apartments, Across from the Central Post Office on Market Street. I'll try and recall her name and will ask my brothers if they remembered her name.

    This all took place during the late 1960s someone may look up that title in the library and come across it. It may have where Lindberg stayed while in St. Louis.
