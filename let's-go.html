ST. JAMES CHURCH PUBLICATION

LET'S GO

Beginning in 1925, and continuing with disruptions over several periods of time, St. James Parish has published an informative church bulletin called "Let's Go" and occasionally under other names. Below are various "sets" of these bulletins.



  • The first set: 1925-1932

  • The second set: late 1940s and early 1950s
