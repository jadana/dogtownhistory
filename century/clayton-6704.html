DOGTOWN CENTURY HOMES

6704 Clayton Ave.

Certified as built in 1878
Current residents 2003: Viola Pisoni and Betty Seidner

More information beneath the photo

Theodore Hubbard is listed as living there in 1900.

The current (2003) owners believe it dates back to the Civil War times.

Pre-1900 maps don't seem to show this house at all.

Primary researcher: Jim Glaser
