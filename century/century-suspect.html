SUSPECTED CENTURY HOMES IN DOGTOWN WHICH HAVE SOME PROBLEMS IN VERIFING THEM

Below is a list of homes which meet two of the three criteria the century committee recognizes:

  1. They are listed in the 1900 census as having occupants in 1900.
  2. They are listed appear to the eye to be old enough to be the home in the 1900 census.

The committee's third "initial" criteria is that the candidate home have some support for century status in St. Louis City records.

The homes below do not have that support in the "simple" on-line search, and thus will need further research to find this support before becoming candidates for verification by the Century Home Committee.
