DOGTOWN CENTURY HOMES

1547 Tamm Ave.

Certified as built in 1903 Current owner: Mohammad Saffaf

Bob Corbett adds a note: A couple of years ago, I believe it was 2000, I passed this house one day and saw two men working on it. I went over to chat. It was Mohammad Saffaf and his son. They were totally rehabing the house. They had kept the shell and some of the basic walls, but gutted other parts. They then sort of slowly rebuilt the building over the old shell.

Primary researcher: Jim Glaser
