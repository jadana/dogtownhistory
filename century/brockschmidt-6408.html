Certified as built in 1902
Current residents 2003: Adrian Mrozewski

More information beneath the photo

August Leibmann is listed as living at this address in 1900 and back to 1896.

This house is the first in a row of 7 or 8 idendical houses. It would seem they would all be build at the same time.
