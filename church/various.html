VARIOUS PHOTOS OF ST. JAMES CHURCH

This photo, found at St. James rectory, is undated. However, I believe it to be a very early photo. First of all, it is fairly clear that the rectory had no yet been built. This view could not have been shot without including parts of the rectory. The rectory was built in 1950-51 so it is earlier than that.

Notice that at the right corner of this photo is the "old" St. James School -- the one built in 1902.

In a different place, I have cut out this section of the photo and enlarged it to give us a partial view of this rarely photographed school.
