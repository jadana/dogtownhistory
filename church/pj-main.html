PATRICK J. O'CONNOR -- MAIN PAGE

Born January 1, 1883 in Ballybunion, Kerry County Ireland
Ordained June 9, 1907, St. Patrick's College Carlow, Ireland
Became an assistant at St. James the Greater 1912
In 1916 he took over the parish as "administrator" with all the duties of pastor
He was formally named pastor in 1920
Died July 30, 1952
