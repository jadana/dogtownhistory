OLD ST JAMES RECTORY

Photo of the Old Rectory taken in about 1942.

This photo was found by Miss Battaglia, principal of St. James at the school. However, this is a "Corbett family" photo. The two girls are Pat and Kathleen Corbett, daughters of Bill and Olivine Corbett. This photo was taken in the back yard of my Grandmother Corbett, at 6410 Wade Ave. Something just fascinating to me is that the photo shows the rectory only because MY HOUSE -- where I live now -- is not there. My parents built this house where I live (1419 Tamm) in 1949.

I have also done a close up of the rectory from this photo

This rectory was torn down in 1951. See that story here.
