St. James the Greater 1860 --
6401 Wade Avenue
St. Louis, Missouri 63139

Special thanks to Sally Ryan Sharamitaro for bringing me this document

Irish (and Italian) originally predominated.
It is the center of the Dogtown neighborhood, south of Forest Park.
Founded as a mission of St. Malachy's Church in what was once called the 'Cheltenham' neighborhood.
