THE PASTORS OF ST JAMES PARISH



  1. 1861: Father John O’Sullivan (Born in Ireland).

  2. 1861-1866: Father Michael Welby

  3. 1867-1869: Once again a mission of St. Malachy’s.
    (Father Charles Ziegler was pastor of St. Malachy’s from 1868-1869 and would have served St James.)

  4. 1869-1870: Father Thomas Manning
    Also pastor of St. Malachy’s at the same time.

  5. 1870-1878: Father Henry Kelly (Born in Ireland)
    Also pastor of St. Malachy’s at the same time.

  6. 1878-1884: Father Thomas Ambrose Butler (Born in Ireland)
    Also pastor of St. Malachy’s at the same time.

  7. 1884-1896: Father Patrick J. McNamee (Born in Ireland)

  8. 1896-1916: Father Edmund A. Casey (Born in Ireland)

  9. 1916: Father Maurice O’Flaherty (Born in Ireland)

  10. 1916-1952: Father Patrick J. O’Connor (Born in Ireland)
    First as administrator and pastor from 1929.

  11. 1952-1965: Father Henry J. Lambert

  12. 1965-1967: Msgr. Rowland E. Gannon

  13. 1967 - 1988: Father Thomas M. Flynn

  14. 1988 - 1990 Father Martin K. Mannion

  15. 1990 – 2002: Father David Roche

  16. 2002 - present: Father John J. Johnson
