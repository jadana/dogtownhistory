VARIOUS PHOTOS OF ST. JAMES SCHOOL BUILDINGS

This photo, found at St. James rectory, is undated. Actually, what is here is one tiny portion of a much larger photo of the church, but I cut the church out to empahsize the partial photo of the "old" school. This is the school which was built in 1902. There aren't many decent photos of this school which I've seen.

The first half the present school was begun in 1949 and opened in Fall 1950. The first number of years it was only part of the school and the 1902 school up the street also housed classrooms.
