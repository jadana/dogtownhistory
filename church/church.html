  • St. James the Greater, first called of the two apostles named James. Feast Day: July 25th

  • A Time-line of important dates in St. James Parish

  • The FIRST St. James Church   St. James was founded as a mission of St. Malachy's Church in 1860. Early on Richard Tobin built the first church and it burned in 1891.

  • The "Old" Church and Rectory in 1897   This is cardboard card which advertised a mission being given at St. James. This is the earliest photo I know of, and contains the boundaries of the parish, which was just huge!

  • The "Old" Church   This was the second church in St. James and was built around 1891. It was torn down in the 1950s.

  • The "Old" Rectory   This was on Tamm Ave. just at the corner of Wade (the east portion of Wade). It was built around 1891. It was torn down in the 1951s.

  • The Bells of St. James Church    A 1928 article in Let's Go traces the bells back to the Old Church.

  • St. James Convent in 1912

  • A Description of who's who in the paintings in St. James Sanctuary    Father Borcic has tried to identy each major historical figure pictured in the tapestries of the back sanctuary.
