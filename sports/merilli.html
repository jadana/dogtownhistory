TONY MIRIELLI: DOGTOWN ROLLER SKATING CHAMPION

Special thanks to Rose Chiaurro Kennedy for the following information and materials

From recent Post-Dispatch, but from a 1943 photo

Tony is a member of the National Roller Skating Hall of Fame and there is a display about his career at the Nation Roller Skating Museum in Lincoln Nebraska.

One of the things Tony did was to design a shoe with miniture wheels for smooth dance skating.
