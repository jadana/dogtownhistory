ST. JAMES TRACK TEAM, 1907

From the left: 1. Jim Gallaher, 2. Frank Signaigo, 3. Max Fournier, 4. Paul O'Gorman, 5. Chip McCone, 6. Gaston McClusky, 7. Ed Burke, 8. Will Gallaher and 9. John O'Gorman.

James F. O'Gorman adds this note to the track photo:

Just for the record, John O'Gorman was my father's (Paul's) first cousin, so he'd be (I guess) my second cousin once removed or something like that, rather than my uncle.

Max was killed in action in France in 1918; John O'G became a builder in St. Louis and Texas; Paul worked for Shapleigh Hardware Co. most of his life; Jim Gallaher was killed in an auto accident about 1927.
