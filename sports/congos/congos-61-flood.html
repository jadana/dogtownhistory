DOGTOWN MEMORIAL CONGREGATIONAL CHURCH CONGOS BASEBALL TEAM 1961; WITH CURT FLOOD: KHOURY LEAGUE NATIONAL CHAMPIONS

Special thanks to Robert Messel for this photo and the names





This is the Atom Class “C” Khoury League National Champions and the photograph was taken in the basement of Memorial Congregational Church.

Left to right front row Nick Pappagianis, Vernon (Butch) Dudas, Walter Yeager, Robert Messel, David Ashley, Don Eggleston

Middle row Robert (Corky) Carter, Dennis Doyle, Paul Zellmer, Kevin Zellmer, Paul Kotis, Richard Rice, Mike Kelly, Bobby Wiendrick

Back row Cardinal center fielder Curt Flood.
