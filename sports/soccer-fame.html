DOGTOWN MEMBERS OF THE ST. LOUIS SOCCER HALL OF FAME

The 26 members are listed below in order of year of acceptance

The Dogtown Historical Society has had a bench donated in honor of the men of Dogtown who are members of the St. Louis Olde Typers Soccer Hall of Fame. The 26 members are listed below in order of year of acceptance and elementary school if known.

  1. Edward Hart (1972)
    St. James class of 1934

  2. Jim Nolan (1972)
    St. James class of 1913

  3. John Rooney (1972)
    St. James School
  4. Bob Corbett (1975)
    St. James class of 1931
    Note to families of other members: See this link to my father's site about his soccer career. If you have photos and other data on your own member, I will work with you to mount a page for that player so that photos, stories and such may apprear as it does for my own father.
