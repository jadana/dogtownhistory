HIBERNIANS INVADE CLAYTON

Clayton Citizen
page 1
March 21, 1984

Visiting Irish Cousins: All dressed in look alike Irish "fisherman net" sweaters the O'Connor clan proudly marches in the parade behind their family crest. Dr. and Mrs. Robert O'Connor from Florissant march proudly with their visiting cousins from County Cork, Ireland, Mr. and Mrs. Danjoe O'Connor

Riding the side of a U. City fire engine sponsored by the Ladies Auxiliary Clowns, Mr. and Mrs. Allan Di Rie of St. John wave at the 2000 Clayton parade watchers, the first Clayton parade sponsored by the Ancient Order.
