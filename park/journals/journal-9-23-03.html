2 nine hole courses open at Forest Park: one to come

September 23, 2003
By Bob Corbett

Folks, today on my bike ride I saw two engineers on the golf course. I rode over to ask some questions about the course and one of them had a score card with him which has the diagram.

I put this all on my web site with some explanations. Hope it makes sense. I also tried to give you some idea of the third and final nine that is coming, but I had to do that in terms of the old 18 hole course, which I know many of you do know.
