LINDELL PAVILON CAFE MENU

Inital menu: opening menu, November, 2003
Hours: 6:30 AM -- 4 PM, 7 days a week

A note on seating: There are FOUR seating areas:

  1. In the small cafe itself -- about 4-5 tables.
  2. In the very large well-lit high ceilinged lobby (many tables)
  3. The front porch of the building (facing the Muny Opera)
  4. The back side of the building (facing the handball courts and racket ball courts)
Thus one can have sun or shade or indoor seating any time of day!!!!
