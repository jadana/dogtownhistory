LIVING CREATURES SEEN IN FOREST PARK

Not including humans, insects, fish or things seen in the zoo

This is a working document. As we add to it I hope to add photos and details. Contribtions are welcome. Sightings must be DATED and after the date below this line.

Last updated July 28, 2006

Folks, I hope to build this list of actually sightings beginning from this point forward, and recorded by date. I use several categories of frequency: daily, often (just short of daily), scarce and rare. With the exception of "daily" the others are not very precise, but I think they are workable. I just started with what I'd seen today and yesterday, or had in some of my notes. I have probably missed some of the "daily" ones on this first listing, but I thought it might to fun to have this.

Also, I invite you to send me digitial photos of things YOU actually photographed in Forest Park. Photos must include date the photo was taken.

Join in the fun of building the list.



The list was begun 7/28/04 though a couple of sightings noted were before that date.
