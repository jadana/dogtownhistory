CLASS OF 1960 IN KINDERGARTEN -- 1952

This was a very large class and the kindergarten seems to have been divided into two. The first picture was taken when Father O'Connor was still well enough to be in the photo, so that must have been in the early winter of 1952. By the second photo he was not in the photo, and I suspect added at a later date.

This first photo appeared in the March 30. 1952 edition of Let's Go.
