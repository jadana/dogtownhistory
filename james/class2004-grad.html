CLASS OF 2004 -- GRADUATION CLASS PHOTO

Class names and other data are beneath photo

This is the 100th graduation class of St. James School. The school opened in 1902 and the first graduation class was 1904. That would have made 2003 the 100th graduation class, however, there were no graduates in 1905. Every other year since has had a graduation class, and now the 100th class.

The class members are:

Top row: Principal, Miss Battaglia, 1. Mark Saunders, Chris Poelker (faculty), 2. Nathan Alvernia, 3. Kenneth Dusold, Father Johnson.

Middle row: 1. Kayla Wright, Sarah Smid, Holly Stacey, Kristin Freeze

Front row: 1. Michael Waterhouse, 2. Savannah Myers, 3. Kelly Cordia, 4. Stephanie Cedeno, 5. Brittany Edwards, 6. Anthony Richie, 7. Christopher Dirnberger.
