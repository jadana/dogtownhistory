CLASS OF 1970 - 30TH REUNION

The class of 1970 met at Shrewsbury community center for a 30th class reunion gathering and party. Below are three collages of photos which Jim Keane took. In sum it is actually 10 photos. Hope you all enjoy seeing yourselves again!!

Jim Keane (at: James Keane: jak011656@worldnet.att.net) says of this first collage: "3 group pictures: too many disorganized faces to try and give them all names.

Middle collage:

  • Top picture: Ray Bozesky.
  • Middle picture left to right: Joe Morgan and Tom Scherzinger.
  • Bottom picture left to right: Ray Bozesky, Joe Morgan, Mrs. Hallie Witte, Tom Scherzinger, and Mary Witte Bentrup.

Bottom collage:

  • Top left picture from left: Joe Ortwerth, Tom Gaffney and Bob Andert.
  • Top right picture from left: Mrs. Peggy Griffin Lake, and Jim Keane.
  • Bottom left picture from left: Mrs. Peggy Griffin Lake and Joyce Dietl Hunter.
  • Bottom right picture from left: Cheryl Murphy Brandt, and Joe Donaldson.

It was a delight for me (Bob Corbett) to see my old friend, neighbor and childhood playmate, Peggy Griffin, in the photos. Peggy had taught the class of 1970 in kindergarten. While Peggy was just a couple of years ahead of me at St. James, there were many a great game of hide-and-seek that we all shared up on the corner of Wise and Childress in the 1940s as we were growing up.

Bob Corbett corbetre@webster.edu.com
