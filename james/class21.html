CLASS OF 1921

Students: 17 (11 boys and 6 girls)

I have never yet seen a graduation photo of this class. If anyone can help me locate one to scan I would be most appreciative.

However, special thanks to Kevin McDermott for lending me this photo of one of the lower class photos of this class. His grandmother, Margaret Wiber is on this photo and is a member of the class of 1921. My guess is this might be a 5th or 6th grade photo.

Class names of the graduating member of this class and other data are beneath photo

Front row: 1. William O'Shaughnessy.

Row 2: 1. Joseph Ryan, 5. Margaret Wiber

The class list is below.

Students in the class: Clarence Adler, Dennis Coad, Pauline Epstein, Marie Gibbons, Bernard Huff, Veronica Kelly, Michael Newport, Vince O'Gorman, William O'Shaughnessy, Anthony Palumbo, Cecil Peppers, Henry Riddle, Joseph Ryan, Mary Syron, Edward Thiele, Irene Valler, and Margaret Wiber.
