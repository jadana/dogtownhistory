CLASS OF 1920

Students: 27 (16 boys and 11 girls)

Class names and other data are beneath photo

A special thanks to Mary Gruner Schmitz for the loan of this photo and to Bill Vorbeck for scanning it for me.

The class list is below. However, I only have one student matched with the photo. Any help which you may give, please e-mail me name and photo match-ups.

Top row: 4. Mario Gioia, last boy on right is John O'Shaughnessy.

Walter Barr, Colleen Bawn, Josephine Berry, Ada Bolt, Margaret Coad, Arthur Curlee, Elizabeth Dressel, Dennis Dwyer, James Egli, John Egli, Edmund Fannen, Mario Gioia, James Ianuzzi, Virginia Jones, Jane Lynch, Anthony Matreci, Ruth May, Dorothy McKenna, Agnes McLaughlin, Charles McVey, Martin Moore, Frank Morrissey, Francis O'Shaughnessy, Ferdinand Ritter, August Sensenbrenner, and Rosemary Wiss.

Two boys who are listed as graduating are not on this photo.
