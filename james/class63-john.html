CLASS OF 1963 -- JOHN CORBETT AT AWARD CEREMONY

John Corbett won an award in 1963 from the Amvets in their essay contest. John pointed our to me just recently that he was greatly influenced by the writing of Father P.J. O'Connor in what he had written in his prize winning essay on patriotism.

John Corbett receiving Amvets award from Frank Byrne.
