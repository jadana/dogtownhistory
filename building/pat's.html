PAT'S BAR AND GRILL

Special thanks to Joe Finn, current owner of Pat's Bar and Grill, for the loan of this, what he believes may be the earliest surviving menu of Pat's. When I scanned it, I did have to write in some prices which were done on the menu in hand and were a bit higher than the original prices on the menu, but I went with the one's actually on this menu.

As best we can tell this is a menu from the early to mid 1940s. Pat's opened in 1942.
