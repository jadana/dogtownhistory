DOLAN'S CLIFTON HEIGHTS HOTEL
AND JOHN P. DOLAN'S SALOON: TAMM AND MANCHESTER

Dolan's saloon was serving Phoenix lager beer

Before Dolan Realtors came to be, the building was a hotel/tavern run by the Dolan Family. One of the younger Dolans began to use a back room to sell realestate and the rest is, as they say, history. This photo must be around the turn of the century or early part of the twentieth century.
